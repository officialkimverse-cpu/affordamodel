How Affordamodel works
01
Set up your project

Enter your site details, unit count, AMI mix, and financing context. This becomes your Scenario 0 baseline — the foundation every future assumption change is measured against.

02
Test assumptions in plain English

Type things like "increase tax abatement to 20%" or "change vacancy to 7%". Affordamodel interprets it, updates the pro forma, and explains the impact on your IRR and equity gap.

03
Track every scenario

Every change is logged to a live chart. See exactly how outputs shift as assumptions evolve. Export your full assumption history when you're done.
